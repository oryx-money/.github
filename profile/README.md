# The AI-Native Treasury OS for Decentralized Organizations

Budget enforcement before transactions execute — not months later in a spreadsheet. Oryx is a non-custodial treasury management system built natively on Safe, designed for DAOs managing serious capital.

Multi-chain portfolio tracking, governance-linked budgets, and audit-ready reporting — without ever taking custody of your funds.

## Highlights

- **Non-custodial** — Safe-native, your keys stay yours
- **Multi-chain** — Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Polygon
- **Budget enforcement** — Budget vs. actual tracking linked to on-chain governance
- **MCP-native** — AI-powered treasury queries and reporting
- **Audit-ready** — Exportable, verifiable, compliant
